About Vuteq


Bussines

Check our Bussines

Assembly

The process of combining various components into a single, complete product. This stage includes assembling the interior and exterior parts.

Injection

The process of molding plastic components using an injection molding machine. The plastic material is heated until it melts, then injected into a mold to form the component according to the design.

Sewing

The process of sewing fabric, synthetic leather, or other soft materials for vehicle interior components.

Carton Box

Carton boxes are used as protective packaging for automotive components before they are sent to customers or assembly departments.

Pallet Design

Pallet design is the process of designing a shipping base or pallet so that automotive components can be stacked safely and efficiently.

Packing & Transportation

This stage includes the final packaging (packing) and transportation of the product to the customer or to the production line.

GM Warehouse & Management

The GM Warehouse section is responsible for the storage, management, and distribution of general materials and production components.
