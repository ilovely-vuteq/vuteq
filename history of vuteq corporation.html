History Of Vuteq Corporation

Vuteq In September 1965, Vuteq Corporation established a company engaged in logistics including windshield assembly services for the Toyota Manutacturing Corporation of Japan. Vuteq Corporation contributes actively as a business partner of several leading companies in lapan, ranging from the automotive and construction industry, housing, until the petroleum industry including Asahi Glass.

As the development of the automotive industry, Vuteq Corporation set up a company in Indonesia in 2004, under the name of PT. Vuteq Indonesia. Vuteq in Indonesia has an important role in the development of the automotive industry. where Vuteq has the advantage of producing such parts manufacturing interior and exterior of the car, assembly services automotive parts such as tires and windshield assembly, including the manufacture of packaging such as pallets and cardboard boxes.

Vuteq is committed to contribute actively to the advancement of technology and competitive automotive market, with the slogan “Developing the Future with Passion and Sincerity”Vuteq committed will always produce a quality product with the “Always New Challenges”.
