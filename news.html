News About Vuteq

The Vuteq Group Directives 2024 carries the slogan "Let's Level Up Together — Bright, Fun, and Reliable Work" or in Japanese "明るく楽しく確かな仕事 仲間と共にレベルアップ".

The company's main focus for 2024 encompasses six key points. First, the comprehensive implementation of safety, health, and disaster prevention management, based on the principle that safety is the key to everything. Second, quality improvement, as good quality is the foundation of customer trust and satisfaction. Third, teamwork development through active communication to create a collaborative work environment. Fourth, improving customer service by increasing satisfaction, developing team member capabilities, implementing flexible production systems, and improving technology and workplace comfort. Fifth, promoting IT resources and digitalization to increase efficiency and productivity. Sixth, corporate social responsibility, by implementing business compliance and a positive approach to environmental protection.

With the spirit of “Developing the Future with Passion and Sincerity,” Vuteq is committed to building a future full of integrity and a spirit of togetherness.
